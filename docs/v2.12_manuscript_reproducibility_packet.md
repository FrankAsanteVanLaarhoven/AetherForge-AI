identified root causes. Reviewers value controlled negative results when clearly interpreted.

## Main claim

> Verified memory retrieval is load-bearing for this code-agent, contributing +17.8 pp
> over the adapter alone. Retraining, global repair-memory promotion, and TF-IDF routing
> do not improve over the champion configuration. The binding constraint is retrieval
> relevance: TF-IDF similarity cannot distinguish algorithm-specific repair relevance
> from vocabulary-family overlap. The oracle upper bound for index-selection routing is
> 23/32 = 71.9%, leaving 9 tasks unsolvable by any routing of the current indexes.

## Not allowed claims

- SWE-bench success (not evaluated)
- Production-grade reliability (28 tasks, best-of-3 is probabilistic)
- The 96.4% diagnostic as a clean held-out champion
- General superiority without co-evaluation on the same tasks
